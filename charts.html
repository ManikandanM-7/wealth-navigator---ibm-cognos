Financial Insights Dashboard

Debt-to-Equity vs Funding
Visualizes the company's debt-to-equity ratio against funding sources across fiscal years. This helps stakeholders understand financial leverage and the company's risk level. The higher the debt-to-equity ratio, the more the company relies on debt for growth.
Expense vs Funding Trend
Tracks monthly expenses and funding inflows to guide budget planning and alignment. Monitoring this trend can help ensure the business is not overspending while maintaining adequate funding sources. This analysis provides actionable insights for long-term sustainability.
Operating Costs vs Funding
Combines bar and line data to compare operating costs with funding monthly. By analyzing the interaction between funding and operating costs, we can identify any cash flow gaps and take corrective actions to improve liquidity.
Financial Health by Company
Displays a pie chart showing the financial health and contribution of each company. It allows users to compare companies' debt-to-equity ratios and determine which are in better financial shape for potential mergers or investments.
